**Source**: Heimberg CBT protocol + DCS (D-Cycloserine) study protocols for social anxiety exposure therapy

## Speech Topics — Heimberg Protocol (personal content, lower threat)
1. Telling people about a hobby
2. Telling people about their job
3. Telling about a recent vacation trip
4. Telling about personal history
5. Book report to undergraduate literature class
6. Presentation as grad student in seminar
7. Paper at scientific meeting
8. Toast at wedding/anniversary party
9. Treatment plan at staff meeting
10. Opinion on controversial topic at discussion group
11. Answering questions in class/seminar
12. Committee report at business meeting
13. Reading from magazine/newspaper/religious text
14. Self-introducing at AA/self-help group
15. Chairing an AA meeting
16. Training others in technical procedures
17. Workshop in area of expertise
18. Telling a story at a party

## Speech Topics — DCS Study (controversial, higher threat)
Same-sex marriage, law enforcement profiling, cloning, bilingual education, sex education, gays in military, drug testing at work, welfare reform, arranged marriages, surrogate mothers, polygamy, social security privatization, steroid use in sports, medical ethics, biological/chemical weapons, abortion, airline safety, alcohol and driving, US healthcare, assisted suicide, death penalty, global warming, gun control, immigration, Iraq war, drug legalization, seat belt laws, nuclear power, highway speed limits, stem cell research

## Conversation Topics — Heimberg Protocol (requires ≥2 people)
1. Group conversations at parties (3+ people)
2. Running into a friend at store, making small talk
3. Friend at store with unfamiliar third person
4. Yard work + neighbor chat
5. Classmate conversation before/after class
6. Talking to child's friend's parents
7. Small talk with coworkers during break/cafeteria
8. Introducing yourself to someone new
9. Small talk with strangers in line/waiting areas
10. Starting new job, meeting colleagues
11. Getting to know spouse's friends/colleagues
12. Conversing on a date
13. Asking/answering personal questions
