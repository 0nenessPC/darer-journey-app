**Source**: Evidence-based exposure therapy activities for social anxiety

| Fear Category | Exposure Activities |
|---|---|
| **Being in public** | Shop in crowded store; Attend concert/sporting event; Read in public; Exercise at public gym; Walk in public making eye contact |
| **Center of attention** | Answer question in class/seminar; Drop change in public; Speak loudly; Wear unusual clothing; Join aerobics class |
| **Interacting with others** | Speak to strangers; Ask for time; Chat with store employees; Attend social event; Invite others over; Phone friends; Join club |
| **Performing in front of others** | Exercise in crowded gym; Give speech; Make toast; Eat alone in crowded restaurant; Speak up at meeting; Job interview |
| **Displaying anxiety reactions** | Interact while stuttering/pausing/mumbling; After exercise (simulated sweating); With visibly trembling/shaking hands |
| **Conflict** | Return item at store; Send food back at restaurant; Drive slowly in left lane; Pretend to forget money at purchase; Slowly use ATM/self-checkout |
| **Appearing foolish** | Ask directions to obvious location; Order item not on menu; Incorrectly answer question in public; Mispronounce words intentionally; Slip/fall in public; Drop coins/drink; Attempt purchase without sufficient money; Be intentionally boring in conversation; Call out floors while riding elevator |
